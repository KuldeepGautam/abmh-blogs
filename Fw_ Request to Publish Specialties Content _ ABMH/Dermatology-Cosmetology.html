Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs)

1. What skin conditions are treated at Aditya Birla Hospital’s dermatology department?

We treat acne, eczema, psoriasis, vitiligo, skin allergies, nail disorders, warts, pigmentation, and more. Our team of the best dermatologists in the Pimpri-Chinchwad region handles acute and chronic skin issues for all age groups.

2. When should I see a dermatologist?

You should consult a dermatologist if you have persistent skin issues like rashes, acne, unexplained itching, hair loss, or changes in moles or pigmentation.

3. What causes acne, and how can it be treated?

Hormonal changes, stress, diet, and clogged pores cause acne. Treatments include oral medications, chemical peels, and laser therapies.

4. Does Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital provide treatment for eczema, psoriasis, and other chronic skin conditions?

Yes, we offer long-term management for chronic skin conditions using topical treatments, systemic therapy, and light-based therapies.

5. What cosmetology services are available at Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital?

Our cosmetology department offers facial rejuvenation, laser hair removal, anti-ageing treatments, PRP therapy, body contouring, and more.

6. Do you offer laser treatments for hair removal or pigmentation?

Yes, we use the latest Q-switched and Nd:YAG laser for pigmentation and advanced systems for laser hair removal.

7. What are the best treatments for acne scars or pimple marks?

Depending on severity, treatments may include PRP therapy, dermaroller, chemical peels, or laser resurfacing.
