Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs) on Pediatrics Department

1. What pediatric services are offered at Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital?

ABMH provides comprehensive pediatric care, including Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU), Pediatric Intensive Care (PICU), Pediatric Emergency, Pediatric Neurology, Cardiology, Gastroenterology, Hematology, Immunology, Rheumatology, and Organ Transplant Services.
