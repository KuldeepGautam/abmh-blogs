2. How do I book an appointment with a neurologist or neurosurgeon at ABMH?

3. What are the signs that I should see a neurologist?

Consult a neurologist if you experience frequent headaches, dizziness, numbness, seizures, memory loss, tremors, or difficulty in movement, speech, or coordination. Early diagnosis ensures better treatment outcomes.

4. What are the common neurological disorders treated at ABMH?

We treat stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson’s, multiple sclerosis, brain tumors, neuropathies, and spinal disorders. Our specialists provide personalized care for both acute and chronic neurological conditions.

5. What types of brain surgeries are performed at ABMH?

ABMH offers brain tumor surgery, epilepsy surgery, deep brain stimulation (DBS), aneurysm clipping, and minimally invasive neurosurgery to treat a wide range of neurological conditions with precision and safety.
