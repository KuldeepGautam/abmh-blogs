Overview: Best Stomach Specialist Hospital In Pimpri, Pune

The Gastroenterology Department at Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of disorders related to the digestive system, including the esophagus, stomach, intestines, liver, pancreas, and gallbladder. Our team of highly skilled gastroenterologists, hepatologists, and endoscopy specialists ensures comprehensive care for a wide range of gastrointestinal conditions, using state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic techniques.

Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs) on the Gastroenterology Department

1. What conditions does the Gastroenterology Department at ABMH treat?

ABMH treats acid reflux, gastritis, IBS, Crohn’s disease, ulcers, liver disease, pancreatitis, gastrointestinal cancers, and other digestive disorders with expert care.

2. What diagnostic tests are available at the Gastroenterology Department?

We offer endoscopy, colonoscopy, ERCP, manometry, liver function tests, ultrasound, CT scans, and advanced imaging for accurate diagnosis of GI conditions.

3. Does ABMH provide treatment for liver diseases?

Yes, we specialize in hepatology services, managing hepatitis, fatty liver, cirrhosis, and liver failure, along with advanced liver transplant care.

4. Does ABMH provide emergency care for gastrointestinal issues?

Yes, our 24/7 emergency department handles severe abdominal pain, GI bleeding, food poisoning, and other urgent stomach-related conditions with immediate care.

5. Are pediatric gastroenterology services available at ABMH?

Yes, we have a dedicated Pediatric Gastroenterology Unit offering specialized care for acid reflux, food allergies, liver diseases, and digestive disorders in children.

6. How can I book an appointment with a gastroenterologist?

You can book an appointment with a gastroenterologist at ABMH through our website or by calling our patient services team directly at 9881123006.
