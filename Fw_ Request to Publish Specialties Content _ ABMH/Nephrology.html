Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs) on the Nephrology Department

1. What nephrology services are available at ABMH?

ABMH provides comprehensive nephrology care, including diagnosis and treatment for chronic kidney disease (CKD), kidney stones, hypertension-related kidney disorders, and end-stage renal disease. We also offer dialysis, kidney transplant evaluations, and post-transplant care.

2. When should I see a nephrologist?

You should consult a nephrologist if you experience persistent swelling, high blood pressure, blood in urine, frequent kidney infections, or declining kidney function. Early detection of kidney disease can prevent severe complications.

3. What types of dialysis are offered at ABMH?

We offer both hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis for patients with kidney failure. Our advanced dialysis unit ensures safe and efficient treatment under expert supervision.

4. Does ABMH offer kidney transplant services?

Yes, ABMH provides kidney transplant services, including pre-transplant evaluation, donor matching, surgery, and comprehensive post-transplant care to ensure long-term success.

5. How do I book an appointment with a nephrologist at ABMH?

You can book an appointment with a nephrologist at ABMH through our website or by calling our patient services team directly at 9881123006.
