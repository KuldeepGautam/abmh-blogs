2. When should I consult a rheumatologist?

You should consult a rheumatologist if you experience:

  • Persistent joint pain, swelling, or stiffness
  • Fatigue or muscle weakness
  • Morning stiffness lasting over 30 minutes
  • Unexplained rashes, fever, or weight loss
  • Family history of autoimmune disease

Early diagnosis ensures effective rheumatology & arthritis treatment in Pimpri Chinchwad and prevents complications.

3. What are autoimmune diseases?

Autoimmune diseases occur when the body’s immune system mistakenly attacks its own tissues. These conditions affect joints, muscles, skin, and internal organs. Common autoimmune diseases include rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, and scleroderma. ABMH’s Department of Rheumatology & Immunology specializes in diagnosing and managing these complex disorders.

4. What are the common symptoms of rheumatic diseases?

Typical symptoms of rheumatic diseases include:

  • Joint pain, redness, and swelling
  • Fatigue and low-grade fever
  • Morning stiffness in joints
  • Skin rashes or dryness in eyes/mouth
  • Difficulty moving or walking

If you notice these signs, consult the best rheumatologist in Pimpri Chinchwad at ABMH for expert evaluation.

5. How is rheumatoid arthritis diagnosed?

Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is diagnosed through a combination of:

  • Medical history and physical examination
  • Blood tests (like rheumatoid factor, anti-CCP, ESR, CRP)
  • Imaging studies (X-rays, ultrasound, or MRI)

Early diagnosis and intervention by ABMH’s rheumatologists ensure better disease control and improved quality of life.
