What Is This Test?

The Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS) test measures glucose (sugar) levels in the blood after fasting for at least 8 hours. This test helps in diagnosing and monitoring diabetes and prediabetes. If you are looking for a Fasting Blood Glucose Test in Pune, this test is widely available at diagnostic centers and labs across the city.

Why Is This Test Done?

Doctors recommend this test to:

  • Diagnose diabetes or prediabetes
  • Monitor blood sugar levels in people with diabetes
  • Assess risk factors for diabetes, especially in individuals with a family history of the condition
  • Check for gestational diabetes in pregnant women

What Parameters Are Covered?

The test measures:

  • Fasting Blood Glucose Level – The amount of sugar in the blood after an overnight fast

How to Prepare for This Test?

To ensure accurate results:

  • Fast for 8-12 hours before the test (only water is allowed)
  • Avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours prior to the test
  • Inform your doctor about any medications you are taking
  • Wear a short-sleeved or loose-sleeved shirt for convenience during the blood sample collection

For those in PCMC, this test is available at multiple locations, and you can easily find options by searching for Fasting Blood Glucose Test in PCMC.

Who Should Get This Test Done?

You should consider this test if you:

  • Experience symptoms such as excessive thirst, frequent urination, unexplained weight loss, or fatigue
  • Have a family history of diabetes
  • Are overweight or have high blood pressure
  • Are above 45 years of age, as diabetes risk increases with age
  • Are pregnant and need to check for gestational diabetes

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

You should seek medical advice if:

  • Your results show higher or lower than normal blood sugar levels
  • You experience symptoms of diabetes or hypoglycemia
  • You have concerns about your risk factors for diabetes
