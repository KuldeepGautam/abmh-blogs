What is this test?

The T3 (Triiodothyronine) Test in Pune is a blood test that checks the level of a hormone called T3 in your body. T3 is produced by your thyroid gland and helps control how your body uses energy. It plays a key role in metabolism, heart rate, and body temperature.

Why is this test done?

The Tri Iodothyronine (T3, Total), Serum Test in PCMC is commonly recommended by doctors if you have symptoms that suggest your thyroid might not be working properly.

You might need this test if you experience:

  • Unexplained weight loss or gain
  • Fatigue or weakness
  • Mood swings or anxiety
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Hair thinning or dry skin
  • Sensitivity to heat or cold

It is often done as part of a thyroid profile that includes T3, T4, and TSH levels.

Parameters Covered

This test measures:

  • Total Triiodothyronine (T3): The complete amount of T3 hormone, including both free (active) and bound (inactive) forms in the bloodstream.

How to Prepare for the Test

  • No fasting is required.
  • Take your regular medications unless advised otherwise by your doctor.
  • Avoid biotin supplements at least 48 hours before the test, as they can interfere with the results.
