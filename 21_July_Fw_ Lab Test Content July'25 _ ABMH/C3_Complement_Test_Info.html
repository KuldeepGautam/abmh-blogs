What is the C3 Complement Test?

The C3 complement test checks the level of Complement 3 (C3) protein in your blood. C3 is an important part of your immune system. It helps defend your body against bacteria, viruses, and other harmful substances. If your immune system isn’t functioning properly, or if it's overactive (as seen in autoimmune diseases), your C3 levels can be abnormal. This simple blood test helps detect and monitor such conditions. If you're searching for a C3 Complement Test in Pune, this test is commonly recommended to assess immune system function.

Why is this test done?

  • Assess the functioning of your immune system
  • Help diagnose autoimmune diseases like lupus or rheumatoid arthritis
  • Monitor kidney-related disorders caused by immune issues
  • Identify causes of frequent or chronic infections
