What is this phosphorus test?

The Phosphorus, Inorganic - Serum Test in Pune measures the amount of phosphorus (a mineral) in your blood. Phosphorus is essential for healthy bones and teeth, energy production, and maintaining a proper balance of minerals in your body.

Why is this test done?

Doctors may suggest this test to:

  • Evaluate bone health
  • Check for kidney problems
  • Monitor calcium and phosphorus balance
  • Diagnose certain hormonal issues
  • Investigate symptoms like muscle weakness or fatigue

Parameters Covered:

The test primarily measures the inorganic phosphorus level in the blood.

How to prepare for this test?

  • Usually, no special preparation is needed.
  • You may be asked to fast for a few hours before the test.
  • Inform your doctor about any medicines you are taking.

Who should get this test done?

  • People with symptoms like bone pain, weakness, or confusion
  • Those with known kidney disease or bone disorders
  • Individuals at risk of electrolyte imbalances
  • If advised by your doctor

When to consult a doctor after the test?

After getting your Phosphorus Serum Test in Pimpri Chinchwad, PCMC, or Pune, share the results with your doctor to understand:

  • If your phosphorus levels are within the healthy range
  • Any underlying health issues that may need treatment
  • Dietary or lifestyle changes you may need
