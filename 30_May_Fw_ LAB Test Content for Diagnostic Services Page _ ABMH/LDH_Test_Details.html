What is the LDH Test?

The LDH test measures the level of lactate dehydrogenase, an enzyme found in nearly every cell of your body. When there’s tissue damage, LDH is released into the bloodstream. So, the lactate dehydrogenase test in Pune helps to identify tissue injury or other health concerns.

Why is this test done?

Doctors suggest the LDH serum test in Pimpri, Pune to:

  • Detect and monitor tissue damage
  • Check for infections or diseases affecting the heart, liver, or muscles
  • Track treatment progress for conditions like anemia or cancer

Parameters Covered

This test measures the total LDH enzyme level in your blood.
