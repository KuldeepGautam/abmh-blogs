What is this potassium test?

The Potassium Test in Pune is a simple blood test that measures potassium levels in your blood. Potassium is an essential mineral that helps muscles, nerves, and your heart work properly.

Why is this test done?

This test is commonly done to:

  • Detect if potassium levels are too low or too high
  • Help diagnose kidney, heart, or muscle-related problems
  • Monitor medications that may affect potassium levels
  • Evaluate symptoms such as muscle cramps, weakness, or irregular heartbeat

What parameters are covered?

Potassium level in blood serum or plasma (measured in mEq/L)

How to prepare for this test?

  • Usually, no special preparation is needed
  • Avoid heavy exercise before the test
  • Inform your doctor about any medications you are taking
  • Follow any specific instructions provided by your healthcare provider

Who should get this test done?

  • People experiencing muscle weakness or cramps
  • Those with kidney or heart conditions
  • Patients taking medications affecting potassium
  • Anyone needing routine health monitoring

When to consult a doctor after the test?

  • If potassium levels are abnormally low or high
  • If you experience symptoms like severe weakness or irregular heartbeat
  • For guidance on treatment or medication adjustments
