  • Ferritin: Measures stored iron in your body

What is this test?

The Iron Profile Test in Pune is a blood test that helps measure how much iron is in your blood and how well it’s being used by your body. Iron is important for making red blood cells, which carry oxygen to your organs and tissues. This test checks if your body has too little or too much iron.

Why is this test done?

A doctor may recommend booking an Iron Test near me to:

  • Check for Iron Deficiency Anemia: When your body doesn’t have enough iron
  • Detect Iron Overload: Excess iron can build up in the body and harm organs
  • Monitor Chronic Conditions: Some health issues can affect your iron levels
  • Assess Nutritional Status: Especially if your diet or absorption is a concern

When you book a Full Iron Profile Test in Pune PCMC, you get information on:

  • Serum Iron
  • Total Iron-Binding Capacity (TIBC)
  • Transferrin Saturation
  • Ferritin

These results help give a complete picture of your iron status.

How to prepare for this test?

  • No strict fasting is needed to book your Iron Test in PCMC, Pune, but your doctor may give you specific instructions.
  • Let your doctor know about any medications or supplements you’re taking, as these can affect your test results.

Who should get this test done?

You may want to consider booking a Serum Iron (TIBC) Test PCMC if you’re experiencing:

  • Feeling tired or weak
  • Pale skin
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Brittle nails
  • Inflammation or soreness in the tongue

These symptoms may be signs of low or high iron levels.

When to consult a doctor after the test?

After getting your Iron Profile Test in Pune done, talk to your doctor if:

  • Your iron levels are too high or too low
  • You still feel unwell even with normal results
  • Further testing or treatment is recommended
