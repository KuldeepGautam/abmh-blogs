What Does This Test Include?

  • Measurement of lactate levels in blood or CSF
  • Provides insight into the amount of lactic acid present in the body

What Is This Test?

The Lactate Test Pune measures lactic acid levels in your blood. Lactic acid is produced when your body breaks down sugar for energy, especially when oxygen is low. This test helps doctors understand if your body is getting enough oxygen or if there is a problem with metabolism.

Why Is This Test Done?

  • To detect conditions causing low oxygen levels in the body
  • To monitor illnesses such as sepsis, shock, or heart problems
  • To check for metabolic imbalances involving lactic acid
  • To evaluate muscle or tissue health in critical conditions
  • Sometimes done on cerebrospinal fluid for infections or neurological concerns

Parameters Covered

  • Blood lactate levels (via Lactate Blood Test PCMC or serum)
  • CSF lactate levels, if needed
  • Helps diagnose lactic acidosis and related conditions

How to Prepare for This Test?

  • Usually, no special preparation needed for the Serum Lactate Test PCMC
  • Avoid heavy exercise before the test, as it can raise lactate levels
  • Follow any specific instructions given by your healthcare provider

Who Should Get This Test Done?

  • People with symptoms like fatigue, rapid breathing, or confusion
  • Patients with severe infections, heart, or lung problems
  • Individuals monitored in intensive care units
  • Anyone advised by a doctor to check metabolic or muscle health

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

  • If test results show high lactate levels
  • If you experience dizziness, weakness, or breathing difficulties
  • To discuss next steps based on your Lactate Acid Test Pimpri Chinchwad or Pune test results
