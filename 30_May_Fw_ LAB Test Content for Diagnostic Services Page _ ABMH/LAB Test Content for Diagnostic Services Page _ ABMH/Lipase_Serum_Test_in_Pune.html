What is the lipase serum test?

The Lipase Serum Test measures the level of lipase in your blood. Lipase is an enzyme that helps break down fats in your body. Higher or lower than normal levels of lipase can indicate problems with your pancreas or other digestive issues.

Why is this test done?

  • Pancreatitis (inflammation of the pancreas)
  • Digestive disorders
  • Abdominal pain or other digestive symptoms

What parameters are covered?

This test mainly looks at the lipase enzyme level in your blood, which helps doctors understand how well your pancreas is working.

How to prepare for this test?

  • No special preparation is needed for the Lipase Serum Test in Pune.
  • You may be asked to avoid eating for a few hours before the test, based on your doctor’s advice.

Who should get this test done?

  • Severe stomach pain
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Symptoms of pancreatitis
  • Digestive issues that are persistent

When to consult a doctor after the test?

If your test results show high or low lipase levels, your doctor will explain what it means and may suggest more tests or treatments based on the results.
