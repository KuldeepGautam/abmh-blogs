What is this microalbumin creatinine ratio test?

The Microalbumin Creatinine Ratio Test In Pune is a simple urine test that measures the amount of a protein called albumin in your urine compared to creatinine. Normally, there should be only a small amount of albumin in your urine. When these levels are higher, it may indicate early signs of kidney problems.

Why is this test done?

The Microalbumin Creatinine Ratio Test In Pimpri Chinchwad is often done to:

  • Detect early signs of kidney disease in people with diabetes or high blood pressure
  • Monitor kidney health over time
  • Help diagnose conditions that may cause leakage of albumin in the urine

If you’re looking for a Urine Albumin Creatinine Ratio Test near me in PCMC or Pimpri, this test can help you understand your kidney function.

What parameters are covered?

  • Urine Albumin Level
  • Urine Creatinine Level
  • Albumin-to-Creatinine Ratio (ACR)

How to prepare for this test?

  • No special preparation is needed for the Urine Albumin Creatinine Ratio Test in PCMC or Microalbumin Creatinine Ratio Test In Pimpri.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise just before the test, as it can affect the results.
  • Follow any instructions from your doctor or lab.

Who should get this test done?

  • People with diabetes or high blood pressure
  • Individuals with a family history of kidney disease
  • Those who notice swelling in their feet, ankles, or face

The Microalbumin Creatinine Ratio Test In Pimpri Chinchwad is especially useful for early detection of kidney damage.

When to consult a doctor after the test?

  • If your test results are higher than normal, consult your doctor for a complete evaluation.
  • High albumin levels can be an early sign of kidney issues, so it’s important to discuss the results and possible next steps.
