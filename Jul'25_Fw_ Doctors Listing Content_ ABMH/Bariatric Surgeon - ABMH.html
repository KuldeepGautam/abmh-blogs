Types of Bariatric Surgery Offered

  • Sleeve Gastrectomy – Reduces stomach size to limit food intake
  • Gastric Bypass (Roux-en-Y) – Alters digestive tract to aid in calorie and nutrient malabsorption
  • Mini Gastric Bypass – A simplified, effective version of traditional bypass surgery
  • Revision Bariatric Surgery – For patients needing correction or conversion of previous procedures

All surgeries are performed using minimally invasive techniques, which promote quicker healing, fewer complications, and less postoperative pain.
