What Is a Bicarbonate Test?

A Bicarbonate Test measures the amount of bicarbonate in your blood. Bicarbonate plays a crucial role in maintaining the body's acid-base balance (pH levels), ensuring that various bodily functions work correctly.

If you're looking to book a Bicarbonate Test in Pune, you can easily schedule it online or visit a nearby diagnostic center.

Why Is This Test Done?

Doctors recommend a Bicarbonate Test in PCMC and other locations to:

  • Assess and monitor electrolyte imbalances
  • Diagnose acid-base disorders like acidosis (too much acid) or alkalosis (too much base)
  • Evaluate lung and kidney function, as these organs help regulate blood pH levels

Who Should Get This Test Done?

A Bicarbonate Test in Pimpri Chinchwad may be advised if you:

  • Experience symptoms like weakness, confusion, or breathing difficulties
  • Have a known kidney or lung condition
  • Are undergoing treatment that affects electrolyte balance

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

Once you receive your test results, consult your doctor to:

  • Understand your bicarbonate levels
  • Identify if any further tests are required
