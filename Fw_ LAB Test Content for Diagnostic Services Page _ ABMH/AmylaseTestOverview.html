What is the Amylase Test?

The Amylase Test measures the level of amylase, an important enzyme that helps break down carbohydrates in the body. This enzyme is mainly produced by the pancreas and salivary glands. If you’re experiencing digestive discomfort, your doctor may recommend a Serum Amylase Test in Pimpri or nearby areas to check your enzyme levels.

Why is this Test Done?

  • Identify possible blockages in the pancreatic duct.
