What is a Calcium (Total) Test?

A Calcium (Total) Test measures the overall amount of calcium in your bloodstream. Calcium is an essential mineral that plays a key role in bone health, muscle contraction, nerve function, and heart activity. Maintaining proper calcium levels is crucial for overall well-being.

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

It’s important to consult a doctor if:

  • Your calcium levels are too high (hypercalcemia) or too low (hypocalcemia).
  • You experience persistent bone pain, fatigue, or irregular heartbeats.
  • Your doctor recommends further evaluation based on your results.
