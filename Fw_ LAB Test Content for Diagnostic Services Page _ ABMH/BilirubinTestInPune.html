What is the Bilirubin (Total, Direct, Indirect) Test?

The Bilirubin Test in Pune is a diagnostic test that helps assess liver function and detect conditions related to the liver and blood. Bilirubin is a yellowish pigment produced when red blood cells break down. The liver processes and removes it from the body.

If you're experiencing symptoms like jaundice, fatigue, or abdominal pain, you can book a Bilirubin Test near me at various diagnostic centers in Pune, Pimpri Chinchwad (PCMC), and Chinchwad.


Why is this Test Done?

Doctors recommend this test to:

  • Evaluate liver health and diagnose conditions such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, or liver damage
  • Identify jaundice in adults and newborns
  • Detect bile duct blockages that may cause digestive issues
  • Monitor the progression of liver disease or the effectiveness of treatment
  • Diagnose hemolytic anemia, a condition where red blood cells break down too quickly

Who Should Get This Test?

You should consider a Bilirubin Test in PCMC if you experience:

  • Yellowing of the skin or eyes (jaundice)
  • Dark-colored urine and pale stools
  • Nausea, vomiting, or loss of appetite
  • Abdominal pain or swelling
  • Fatigue or weakness
  • Unexplained fever
  • A history of liver disease or excessive alcohol use

If you’re noticing any of these symptoms, it's important to book a Bilirubin Test near me to rule out potential health concerns.

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

You should consult a doctor if your bilirubin levels are higher than normal, as it may indicate:

  • Liver diseases such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, or fatty liver disease
  • Bile duct obstruction that may require further tests
  • Hemolytic anemia or other blood-related disorders
