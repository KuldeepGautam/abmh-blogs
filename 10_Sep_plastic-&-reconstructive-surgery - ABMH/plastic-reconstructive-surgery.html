Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is plastic and reconstructive surgery?

Plastic and reconstructive surgery involves techniques for restoring the form and function of the body due to defects from birth, injury, disease, or ageing.

2. What is the difference between cosmetic and reconstructive surgery?

Reconstructive surgery focuses on correcting abnormalities to restore function (e.g., repairing a cleft lip or burn injury). Cosmetic surgery, on the other hand, is aimed at enhancing appearance (e.g., rhinoplasty, facelifts). Some procedures may serve both purposes.

Yes, plastic surgery is generally safe when performed by experienced and qualified surgeons under sterile, well-equipped conditions. At Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital, we conduct thorough pre-op evaluations to minimise risks.
