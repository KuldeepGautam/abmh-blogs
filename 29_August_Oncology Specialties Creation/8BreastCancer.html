Breast Cancer Treatment & Procedures

The treatment of breast cancer depends on several factors, including the type and stage of cancer, overall health, age, and patient preferences. At Aditya Birla Hospital, we offer a wide range of evidence-based treatment options to give you the best possible outcome.

  1. Breast Cancer Surgery in Pune – Surgery is often the first step in treating breast cancer. Depending on your condition, our breast cancer surgeons may recommend one of the following:
    • Lumpectomy: Removal of the tumour and a small margin of surrounding tissue, preserving most of the breast.
    • Mastectomy: Complete removal of the affected breast; may be followed by breast reconstruction.
    • Sentinel Lymph Node Biopsy or Axillary Lymph Node Dissection: To check if the cancer has spread to nearby lymph nodes.
    Our surgical team specialises in performing minimally invasive and breast-conserving procedures whenever possible, helping patients maintain both their health and self-esteem.
  2. Medical Oncology (Chemotherapy, Targeted Therapy, Hormonal Therapy) – After surgery or as a standalone treatment, chemotherapy or hormonal therapy may be recommended to destroy any remaining cancer cells. At Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital, we use the latest drug regimens tailored to your cancer type, hormone receptor status, and individual health profile.
  3. Radiation Therapy – High-energy radiation is used to destroy cancer cells that may remain after surgery. Our radiation oncology team uses cutting-edge technology to deliver targeted radiation while minimising damage to healthy tissue.
  4. Breast Reconstruction Surgery – For patients undergoing mastectomy, we offer advanced breast reconstruction techniques performed by skilled plastic surgeons. This can be done immediately after mastectomy or at a later stage, depending on your preferences and health status.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are the early signs of breast cancer?

Early signs may include a lump in the breast or underarm, changes in breast shape or size, nipple discharge, skin dimpling, or pain in the breast. It’s important to consult a breast cancer specialist in PCMC Pune if you notice any of these symptoms.

2. Do I need surgery if I have breast cancer?

Not all breast cancers require surgery. Treatment plans are personalised and may include surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, or a combination. The decision depends on the cancer stage, type, and patient preferences.

3. Is breast cancer curable?

Yes, many cases of breast cancer are curable, especially when diagnosed early. Treatments have advanced significantly, leading to high survival rates and improved quality of life.

4. Where can I find the best breast cancer hospital near me?

Aditya Birla Memorial Hospital in Pimpri Chinchwad is one of the best breast cancer hospitals in Pune, offering advanced diagnosis, treatment, and personalised care under one roof.

5. Can men get breast cancer?

Yes, although rare, men can develop breast cancer. If you’re experiencing symptoms like a lump or nipple changes, it’s important to consult a breast cancer surgeon immediately.
