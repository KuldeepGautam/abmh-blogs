Treatments & Procedures Offered

Our HPB and Surgical Gastroenterology division provides a wide range of treatments for cancers of the digestive system, including:

Surgical Treatments:

  • Esophagectomy – for esophageal cancer
  • Gastrectomy – for stomach cancer
  • Colectomy – for colon and rectal cancers
  • Whipple Procedure (Pancreaticoduodenectomy) – for pancreatic and periampullary cancers
  • Liver Resection (Hepatectomy) – for liver tumors
  • Bile Duct Surgery – for cholangiocarcinoma and gallbladder cancer

Minimally Invasive Surgeries:

  • Laparoscopic and robotic-assisted GI cancer surgeries
  • Keyhole procedures for faster recovery and reduced post-op complications

Supportive Therapies:

  • Neoadjuvant (pre-surgery) and adjuvant (post-surgery) chemotherapy
  • Nutritional support and GI rehabilitation
  • Personalized pain and symptom management

Whether it’s surgery for gastroenteritis, early-stage gastrointestinal tumors, or advanced hepatopancreatobiliary cancers, our approach is tailored, timely, and patient-focused.
