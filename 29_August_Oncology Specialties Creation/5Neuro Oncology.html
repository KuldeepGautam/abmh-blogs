    3. What are the symptoms of a brain tumor?

    Symptoms may include persistent headaches, seizures, nausea, vision problems, or personality changes. Always consult a specialist if you experience any unusual neurological symptoms.

    4. How is a brain tumor diagnosed?

    Diagnosis usually involves MRI or CT scans, neurological exams, and sometimes biopsy procedures to confirm the type and grade of the tumor.
