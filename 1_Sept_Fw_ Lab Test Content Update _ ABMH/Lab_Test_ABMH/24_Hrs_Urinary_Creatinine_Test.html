What is the 24 Hrs Urinary Creatinine Test?

The 24 Hrs Urinary Creatinine Test measures the amount of creatinine excreted in urine collected over 24 hours. Creatinine is a waste product formed by muscles and filtered out by the kidneys. This test helps doctors assess kidney function and detect possible renal issues. Why is this Test Done?

Doctors may recommend this test to:

  • Evaluate how well the kidneys are working
  • Detect early signs of kidney disease
  • Monitor patients with diabetes, high blood pressure, or chronic kidney issues
  • Assess muscle metabolism and related conditions

3. How is this test different from a blood creatinine test?

A blood test checks creatinine in the bloodstream, while this test measures how much creatinine is excreted in urine over 24 hours.
