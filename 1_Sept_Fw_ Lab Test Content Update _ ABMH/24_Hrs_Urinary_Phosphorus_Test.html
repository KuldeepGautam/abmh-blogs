What is the 24 Hrs Urinary Phosphorus Test?

The 24-Hour Urinary Phosphorus Test measures the total amount of phosphorus (phosphate) excreted in urine over a full day. Phosphorus plays an important role in bone strength, energy production, and kidney health. This test helps doctors evaluate how well your body regulates phosphorus and whether your kidneys are filtering it properly. Why is this Test Done?

Doctors may recommend this test to:

  • Assess kidney function and phosphorus balance
  • Detect reasons behind kidney stones
  • Check for parathyroid gland problems
  • Monitor bone health and calcium-phosphorus metabolism
  • Evaluate conditions like vitamin D disorders, malnutrition, or metabolic bone disease
