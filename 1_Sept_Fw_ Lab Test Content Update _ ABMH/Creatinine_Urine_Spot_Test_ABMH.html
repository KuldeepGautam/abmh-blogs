What is the Creatinine - Urine Spot Test?

The Creatinine - Urine Spot Test measures the level of creatinine, a waste product from muscle activity, in a single urine sample. This test helps in assessing how well your kidneys are working. If you’re looking for a URINARY CREATININE SPOT Test in Pune, this is a simple and non-invasive way to monitor kidney function.

Why is this Test Done?

Doctors may recommend this test to:

  • Check overall kidney health
  • Detect or monitor kidney diseases
  • Track the impact of diabetes or high blood pressure on the kidneys
  • Adjust dosages of medicines that are processed through the kidneys

Who Should Get This Test Done?

You should consider this test if you:

  • Have diabetes, high blood pressure, or a family history of kidney disease
  • Are on long-term medications that affect kidney function
  • Need regular kidney health monitoring
  • Have symptoms like swelling, fatigue, or changes in urination

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

Consult your doctor if:
