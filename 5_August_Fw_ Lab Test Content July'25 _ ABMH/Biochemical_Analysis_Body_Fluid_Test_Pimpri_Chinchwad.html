What is the Biochemical Analysis – Body Fluid Test?

The Biochemical Analysis – Body Fluid Test checks for important substances like protein, glucose, and chloride in body fluids such as cerebrospinal fluid (CSF), pleural fluid (lungs), ascitic fluid (abdomen), or synovial fluid (joints). This test helps doctors understand what's happening inside your body, especially when there’s fluid buildup or signs of infection or inflammation. Why is this Test Done?

Doctors recommend this test to:

  • Diagnose infections like meningitis, peritonitis, or pleuritis
  • Detect inflammation or abnormal chemical levels in body fluids
  • Monitor certain medical conditions affecting the brain, lungs, abdomen, or joints
  • Analyze causes of fluid accumulation or swelling
