What is the Apolipoprotein- A1 Test?

The Apolipoprotein-A1 (APO A1) Test measures the level of Apo A1 protein in your blood. This protein plays a key role in removing cholesterol from the body and is linked to “good” HDL cholesterol. If you're looking to assess your heart health, this test can be useful in identifying early risks. You can book an APO A1 Serum Test in PCMC or Pimpri Chinchwad online or at nearby labs.

Why is this Test Done?

Doctors may recommend this test to:

  • Evaluate risk of heart disease or stroke
  • Monitor treatment for cholesterol-related conditions
  • Assess general heart health for people with diabetes, high blood pressure, or a family history of heart conditions

Who Should Get This Test Done?

You should consider this test if you:

  • Have a family history of heart disease
  • Are managing diabetes, obesity, or high cholesterol
  • Experience high blood pressure or smoke regularly
  • Want to monitor your response to cholesterol-lowering treatments

When to Consult a Doctor After the Test?

Speak with your doctor if:

  • Apo A1 levels are unusually low (could signal increased heart risk)
  • Results are combined with other abnormal cholesterol levels
  • You're unsure how the results fit into your overall health status
